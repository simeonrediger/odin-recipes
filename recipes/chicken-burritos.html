Chicken Burritos

Description

A classic Mexican-inspired dish filled with savory, juicy chicken, seasoned rice, black beans, and topped with salsa and cheese. Perfect for a hearty meal!

Ingredients

  • For the chicken:
    • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Juice of 1 lime
  • For the burritos:
    • 4 large flour tortillas
    • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably seasoned with lime and cilantro)
    • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • 1/2 cup salsa or pico de gallo
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken:
    1. In a skillet over medium heat, heat the olive oil.
    2. Season the chicken breasts with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    3. Cook the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
    4. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.
  2. Prepare the burritos:
    1. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet for about 30 seconds on each side.
    2. On each tortilla, layer the rice, black beans, sliced chicken, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and fresh cilantro.
    3. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it up tightly from the bottom to enclose all the fillings.
    4. Slice in half and serve!
