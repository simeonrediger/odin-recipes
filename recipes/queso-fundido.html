Queso Fundido

Description

Queso Fundido is a warm, gooey cheese dip with a bit of spice, often served as a starter or appetizer. It's perfect with tortilla chips or warm tortillas!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound chorizo, casing removed and crumbled
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Chihuahua or Oaxaca cheese (or other melty cheese)
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the chorizo:
    1. In a medium skillet over medium heat, heat the olive oil.
    2. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© for about 2-3 minutes until softened.
    3. Add the crumbled chorizo and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Prepare the cheese:
    1. Reduce the heat to low.
    2. Add the shredded cheeses to the skillet along with the milk (or cream).
    3. Stir constantly until the cheese has melted into a smooth, creamy dip.
  3. Final touches:
    1. Add the crushed red pepper flakes for a little heat (optional).
    2. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
    3. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with fresh cilantro.
    4. Serve with tortilla chips, warm tortillas, or vegetables for dipping!
